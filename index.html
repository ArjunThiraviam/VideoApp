Web Video Player Application

HTML video doesn't require plugins for playback, but the default experience is entirely different across browsers.

This video player app enables us to get a fixed aspect ratio, fully responsive video playback that performs and appears great in all browsers fusing the user experience and allowing custom functionality, such as video description, playlists and full-screen playback.
